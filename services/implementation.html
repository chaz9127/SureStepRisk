Implementation

GRC and analytics solutions don't have to be costly, complex or time consuming to install. SureStep has the knowledge and experience to deploy these solutions efficiently and comprehensively into on-premise systems or a hosted environment in the Cloud. SureStep Risk Management offers Implementation Services which can provide the following services and benefits:

  • Full end-to-end Implementation for most partner software and platforms
  • Business requirements development and solution blueprinting
  • User and role definition
  • Installation and management of on-premise or Cloud environments
  • Spin-up and customization of your Cloud environment
  • Development and deployment of automation functionality
  • Configuration and customization to IBM, VMWare, and Informatica platforms to meet business requirements
  • Development and deployment of Cognos reports and dashboards
  • Development and deployment of mobile apps
  • Content loading
  • Ongoing support via SureStep Cloud Managed Services

Our implementations deliver complete, end-to-end enterprise risk management and compliance platforms for our customers. Your organization will be able to derive value from your enterprise platforms. Our expertise in implementating GRC platforms and specialized data systems has consistently provided successful corporate initatives for our customers.
